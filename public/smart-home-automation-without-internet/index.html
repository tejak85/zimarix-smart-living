What happens to most smart homes when the internet goes down?

Most smart home devices stop responding the moment WiFi drops. Your lights, your AC, your scenes — all frozen until connectivity returns. Some brands go further: if their cloud server goes down, your automations stop working even if your internet is fine.

How Zimarix works differently

Every Zimarix device runs its intelligence locally — on the device itself, not in a data centre somewhere. If WiFi drops, commands route via our server. If internet goes down entirely, Bluetooth takes over automatically. No manual switching. No setup. It just works.
