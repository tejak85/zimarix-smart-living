The problem with most smart home brands

Most smart home automation in Bangalore runs on plastic panels, shared cloud servers, and protocols borrowed from consumer electronics. When the server goes down — or the company behind it shuts one down — your smart home stops being smart.

What Zimarix does differently

Zimarix is engineered for homes where compromise isn't acceptable. Every panel is milled from aircraft-grade aluminium 6061 T6, anodised in our own facility to a consistent 20+ micron finish. The intelligence runs on the device itself — scheduling, automation, intrusion detection — none of it depends on a cloud server.
