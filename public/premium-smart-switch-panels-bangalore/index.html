Product overview

Zimarix panels are not manufactured. They're milled — from aircraft-grade aluminium 6061 T6, the same alloy used in aerospace and premium electronics. Every panel is anodised in our own facility to a consistent 20+ micron finish.

Variants and finishes

Available in three panel sizes (1x3, 1x4, 2x3) and multiple anodised finishes — Graphite, Espresso, Olive, Steel, and Platinum. All compatible with the Zimarix scene controller for whole-home automation.

Haptic feedback. Adjustable LED colour and brightness. IR remote recording. Cluster control. On-device scheduling. Intrusion detection. Temperature monitoring. All from one app.
